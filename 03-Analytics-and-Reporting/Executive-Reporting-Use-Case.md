# Executive Reporting Use Case

## Scenario

Senior leadership requires visibility into business performance, operational efficiency, and sales pipeline health.

## Business Requirement

Provide a consolidated executive dashboard showing:

- Revenue Trends
- Sales Performance
- Lead Conversion Rates
- Quote Conversion Rates
- Customer Metrics
- Operational KPIs

## Expected Outcomes

- Improved decision-making
- Faster identification of risks
- Better resource allocation
- Increased operational transparency

## Business Analyst Contribution

- Gather executive reporting requirements
- Define KPIs and success metrics
- Align reporting with business objectives
- Ensure data consistency and accuracy
- Facilitate stakeholder reviews
