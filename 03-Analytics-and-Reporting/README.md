# Analytics and Reporting

This section highlights my experience and interest in business analytics, KPI reporting, operational visibility, dashboard-driven decision-making, and performance measurement.

## Focus Areas

- KPI Tracking
- Business Reporting
- Dashboard Requirements
- Operational Metrics
- Sales Analytics
- Process Performance Monitoring
- Data-Driven Decision Making

## Business Analyst Perspective

A Business Analyst should be able to translate business requirements into measurable KPIs and reporting solutions that help stakeholders make informed decisions.

## Typical Questions Analytics Helps Answer

- Where are process bottlenecks occurring?
- Which opportunities are most likely to convert?
- How is operational performance trending?
- Which workflows require improvement?
- What actions should business leaders prioritize?
