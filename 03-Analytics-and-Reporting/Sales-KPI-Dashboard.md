# Sales KPI Dashboard

## Business Objective

Provide visibility into sales performance, lead conversion, quote effectiveness, and pipeline health.

---

## Key Metrics

### Lead Metrics

- Total Leads
- Qualified Leads
- Lead Conversion Rate

### Opportunity Metrics

- Open Opportunities
- Opportunity Win Rate
- Opportunity Value

### Quote Metrics

- Quotes Generated
- Quote Acceptance Rate
- Average Quote Cycle Time

### Revenue Metrics

- Revenue Forecast
- Closed Won Revenue
- Average Deal Size

---

## Dashboard Benefits

- Improved sales visibility
- Better pipeline management
- Faster decision making
- Identification of bottlenecks
- Improved forecasting accuracy

---

## Business Analyst Responsibilities

- Gather reporting requirements
- Define KPI calculations
- Validate business rules
- Coordinate with technical teams
- Conduct stakeholder reviews
- Support dashboard testing and adoption
