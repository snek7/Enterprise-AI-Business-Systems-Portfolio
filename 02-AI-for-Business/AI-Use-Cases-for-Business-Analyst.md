# AI Use Cases for Business Analyst Workflows

## Objective

This document summarizes practical AI use cases that are relevant to Business Analyst and Business Systems Analyst roles in enterprise environments.

## Use Case 1: Requirement Summarization

Generative AI can summarize stakeholder discussions, meeting notes, and business inputs into structured requirement drafts.

### Business Value
- Faster documentation
- Better consistency
- Reduced manual effort
- Improved stakeholder alignment

## Use Case 2: User Story Drafting

AI can help convert business requirements into Agile user stories with acceptance criteria.

### Example
As a business user, I want faster access to SAP process documentation so that I can resolve operational questions more efficiently.

## Use Case 3: Support Ticket Analysis

AI can help analyze recurring support tickets and identify common issue patterns across enterprise workflows.

### Business Value
- Faster issue classification
- Better prioritization
- Improved root cause visibility
- Reduced repetitive support effort

## Use Case 4: Reporting Insights

AI can summarize dashboard trends, explain KPI movements, and generate business-friendly insights from reports.

### Business Value
- Faster executive summaries
- Improved decision support
- Better reporting adoption

## Use Case 5: Enterprise Knowledge Retrieval

RAG-based systems can retrieve relevant internal documentation before generating responses.

### Business Value
- More accurate answers
- Faster knowledge access
- Reduced dependency on tribal knowledge
- Better onboarding support

## Business Analyst Perspective

AI is most valuable when it improves productivity, workflow visibility, documentation quality, reporting clarity, and decision-making without removing the need for human validation.
