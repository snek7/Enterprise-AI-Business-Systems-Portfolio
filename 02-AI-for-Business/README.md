# AI for Business

This section showcases my interest and hands-on exposure to enterprise AI use cases, workflow automation, Generative AI, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and intelligent business systems.

My focus is not on building AI models from scratch, but on understanding how AI can improve enterprise productivity, knowledge retrieval, reporting, and business decision-making.

## Key Areas

- Generative AI
- AI Copilots
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Workflow Orchestration
- Agentic AI
- Enterprise Knowledge Management
- AI-Assisted Business Analysis

## Business Perspective

I am particularly interested in how AI can reduce manual effort, improve workflow efficiency, enhance reporting visibility, and support better business decisions across enterprise environments.
