# Lead-to-Quote Business Analysis

## Business Problem

Sales teams often struggle with inconsistent lead follow-up, limited visibility into quote status, and delays in converting opportunities into customers.

The objective is to improve visibility, standardize the sales process, and provide accurate reporting throughout the customer lifecycle.

---

# Current Process

Lead Received
↓
Lead Qualification
↓
Opportunity Creation
↓
Quote Preparation
↓
Customer Review
↓
Negotiation
↓
Order Confirmation

---

# Challenges Identified

- Leads not followed up consistently
- Limited visibility into quote progress
- Manual status tracking
- Delayed customer responses
- Inconsistent reporting across teams

---

# Proposed Improvements

- Standardized lead qualification process
- Automated quote status tracking
- Dashboard visibility for sales managers
- Customer follow-up reminders
- Centralized reporting and KPI tracking

---

# User Stories

### User Story 1

As a Sales Representative,

I want to track lead status throughout the sales cycle,

So that I can prioritize customer follow-ups.

### Acceptance Criteria

- Lead status is visible at all times
- Follow-up dates are tracked
- Open leads can be filtered by priority

---

### User Story 2

As a Sales Manager,

I want visibility into quote progress,

So that I can identify pipeline risks early.

### Acceptance Criteria

- Quote status dashboard available
- Pending quotes identified
- Conversion metrics displayed

---

# Key Metrics

- Number of Leads
- Qualified Leads
- Opportunity Conversion Rate
- Quote Conversion Rate
- Pipeline Value
- Average Quote Turnaround Time

---

# Expected Business Value

- Improved sales visibility
- Better lead management
- Faster quote processing
- Increased conversion rates
- Better reporting accuracy
